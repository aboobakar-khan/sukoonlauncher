## 🧠 Psychology Principles

1. **Loss Aversion** - Blurred preview shows what they're missing
2. **Social Proof** - "Join thousands of users..." messaging
3. **Value Proposition** - Clear benefits, not just features
4. **Visual Hierarchy** - Glowing icon → headline → features → CTA
5. **Scarcity** - "Unlock" language creates exclusivity
